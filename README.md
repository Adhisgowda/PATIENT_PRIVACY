# Heapiq Patient Privacy Policy
*Last Updated: July 2026*

At Heipiq, privacy is a fundamental right. We design our systems around data minimization and user-control principles.

### 1. Source-Level Data Masking & Zero-Knowledge Architecture
We deploy Source-Level Masking to prevent unauthorized data exposure:
*   **Local Encryption:** Your platform passwords, full 12-digit Aadhaar numbers, and Ayushman Bharat Health Account (ABHA) IDs are encrypted and partially blurred locally on your mobile device before they travel across the network.
*   **Cloud Data Minimization:** Our cloud databases only store the strictly necessary, sanitized operational fields required to route bookings and comply with healthcare laws.

### 2. Hardware-Encrypted Security Vaults
Your electronic medical records, uploaded prescriptions, and health history are isolated inside a dedicated hardware-encrypted security vault. Even in cases of device theft, your health profiles remain completely unreadable unless unlocked with your specific biometrics or secondary cryptographic vault passcode.

### 3. ABDM Consent Manager Integration
In strict accordance with India's Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act and ABDM compliance:
*   **Consent-Led Sharing:** When utilizing the ABDM "Scan & Share" feature to register at government or private hospitals, your data is packaged into an ephemeral token.
*   **Revocable Access:** You retain granular control over which hospitals can view your ABHA health records, and you can revoke this structural data-sharing permission at any time directly through the app settings.

### 4. Changes to this Privacy Policy
Heapiq may revise this Privacy Policy at any time to reflect changing regulations, security patches, or product updates. Revised documents will be posted directly here with an updated timestamp.
